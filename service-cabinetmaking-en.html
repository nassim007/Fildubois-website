FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I choose the type of wood?

Yes, we guide you in choosing wood species: oak, beech, walnut, pine, lacquered MDF... according to your budget and desired aesthetic.

Do you make furniture from a sketch or photo?

Absolutely. A sketch, an inspiration photo or even a vague idea is enough to get started. We develop the final plans together.

How long does it take to make a custom dressing?

Generally 2 to 4 weeks depending on complexity, plus on-site installation.

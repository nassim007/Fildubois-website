Our story

A trusted craftsman at your service for over 40 years

Every project is unique. We take the time to listen to your needs, understand your space and offer you custom solutions that combine aesthetics, durability and functionality.

Frequently asked questions

Your questions, our answers

How do I get a quote?

Simply contact us by phone, email or via the online form. We arrange a visit to your home or site to assess your project. The quote is provided free of charge and without obligation.

Do you work throughout Belgium?

Yes, Fildubois travels throughout Belgium — Brussels, Wallonia and Flanders. We adapt to your location. For projects further away, travel costs will be specified in the quote.

Do you work on both new builds and renovations?

Absolutely. Our skills cover both new construction and partial or complete renovation projects. Whether you are building your home or looking to modernise your interior, Fildubois is at your service.

Do you handle repairs after damage?

Yes. Our repair interventions are approved by Belgian insurance companies. We draw up quotes in the required format and can communicate directly with your insurer to facilitate claims management.

What timescales should I expect for a project?

Timescales vary depending on the nature and scope of the project. A simple repair can be scheduled quickly, while a custom kitchen requires several weeks of manufacturing. We always provide a precise schedule upon acceptance of the quote.

Who we are

A craftsman, a vocation, a promise of quality

Fildubois is the story of a craftsman who dedicated his life to the art of wood. For over 40 years, I have worked with passion, precision and respect for the client to transform your spaces.

As an independent craftsman, I come directly to your home, without intermediary. Every project is handled personally — from the first visit to the final delivery.

Over the decades, I have completed hundreds of projects for private individuals and professionals: custom kitchens, wooden window frames, sculpted staircases, integrated dressings, unique furniture. Every creation bears my craftsman's signature.

Recognised by Belgian insurance companies, I also intervene for urgent repairs following accidents and damage. The trust of my clients is my greatest reward.

Our values

What sets us apart

Craft expertise

Trained in the old school, I apply traditional techniques enriched by 40 years of experience. Every joint, every finish is carefully handcrafted.

Listening and closeness

I take the time to understand your needs and constraints. Dialogue is at the heart of every successful project. You are never an anonymous file at Fildubois.

Quality without compromise

I select my materials rigorously and only use durable and aesthetic products. Your long-term satisfaction is my primary commitment.

Insurance approved

My interventions are recognised by Belgian insurance companies. I draw up the required quotes and guide you through every step following a claim.

Responsiveness

I respect agreed deadlines and keep you informed at every step. For emergencies, I intervene as quickly as possible to minimise your inconvenience.

All of Belgium

As a mobile craftsman, I travel through all 10 Belgian provinces — Brussels, Wallonia and Flanders — to carry out your projects directly at your home.
